Million Dollar Deviled Eggs

Description

These lemon cooler cookies are a great dessert on a hot summer day, or any time, and they are very easy to make with simple ingredients you might already have on hand.

If you love soft, lemony flavored cookies with just the right amount of sweetness, these cookies are for you!

Ingredients

  • 12 large eggs
  • ¼ cup (55 g) mayo
  • 1 Tablespoon butter softened to room temperature
  • 2 teaspoons yellow mustard
  • 2 teaspoons dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons sweet gherkin pickle juice
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon pepper
  • dash Tabasco sauce optional
  • paprika for sprinkling
  • bacon pieces optional
